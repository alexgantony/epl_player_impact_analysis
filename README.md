# Who Deserved Premier League Player of the Season 2025/26?

Bruno Fernandes won the 2025/26 Premier League Player of the Season award after a record-breaking season. During the campaign he broke the Premier League assist record by setting up 21 goals for his Manchester United teammates.

## Hypothesis

As an Arsenal fan, I personally felt that Declan Rice was more deserving of the award due to his influence on Arsenal's first league title in 22 years and his overall impact throughout the season. However, football is a sport that can be influenced with strong emotions, team loyalty, fan narratives and personal biases.

I did not want my personal opinion and bias to cloud my judgement. So, I wanted to investigate the simple question:

*Who deserved the Player of the Season 2025/26?*

## Objective

The goal of this project is to do a thorough statistical analysis of all the nominees for the Premier League Player of the Season and determine which player has the greatest overall impact during thie 2025/26 season.

## Project Scope

This project will:

- Collect and analyze performance of all nominated player   
- Clean and validate the datasets
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Perform feature engineering additional player performance data
- Develop a custom Player Impact Score
- Compare and rank all official nominees
- Evaluate whether the evidence supports or contradicts my hypothesis.
